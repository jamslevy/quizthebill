About PlopQuiz

We make smart quizzes for smart people.

Our quizzes are particularly smart because nobody writes them. Instead, they are crafted from open scholarly resources such as Wikipedia.

Students can take quizzes and build an academic profile that makes for a great upgrade to a resume. Top achieving students can earn awards and sponsorships.

Sponsors can use PlopQuiz to demonstrate social responsibility and connected with self-motivated learners.


About This Quiz
